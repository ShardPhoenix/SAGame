Run the game by double-clicking minotaur.bat (Windows) or running minotaur.sh (Linux, Mac).


Gameplay instructions are shown in game, on the left side. In brief: arrow keys to move, Ctrl or Space to bomb walls,
grab treasures and extra bombs and run for the exit. You also get a free bomb every 5000 points.

Requires Java. If you don't have Java, you can get it from http://java.com/en/download/inc/windows_upgrade_xpi.jsp

-Background-
You are a greedy adventurer who wants to venture into the maze of the minotaur and steal his treasures. How long can you escape
the minotaur, and how rich will you be when he finally catches you?
